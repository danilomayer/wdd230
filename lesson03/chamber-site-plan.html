Maringa Chamber of Commerce

Danilo Mayer

Overview

Purpose

The Maringa Chamber of Commerce site is being created to facilitate the gathering and collaboration of business owners in the city of Maringa. We promote thriving and healthy capitalism, and support the growth of our comunity.

Scenarios

• What events will the chamber be holding this week that promote business-to-business networking? • Where can I find resources to open my own business? • What has been the average investment for a successful business in the area?
